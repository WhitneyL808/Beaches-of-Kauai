Beaches on Kauai

Kauai has some easy and fun beaches to vist, whether it is with family or friends there are lots of places you can have a good time on, the Garden Isle.

Southside beaches Northshore beaches Lihue Beaches

Southside Beaches

Shipwrecks

  • Activities to do: Bodyboarding/ surfing/hangout
  • Location: Poipu
  • Travel time from Lihue: 20-30 min
  • Overall rating: 8/10- packed with other people,clear water, limited beach parking

Koloa Boat Harbor

  • Activities to do: Cookout/ hangout/ swim
  • Location: Poipu
  • Travel time from Lihue: 20-30 min
  • Overall rating: 7/10- not much to do besides hangout, water can be murky sometimes, decent amount of people

Allertons

  • Activities to do: Bodyboarding/ small hike
  • Location: Poipu
  • Travel time from Lihue: 20-30 min
  • Overall rating: 6/10- sticks and stuff in the water, great hangout spot, not too mcuh people, more secluded

Brenneckes beach

  • Activities to do: Swimming/ cookout/ hangout/ party/ events
  • Location: Poipu
  • Travel time from Lihue: 20-30min
  • Overall rating: 7/10- lots of people, nice sunsets, big open area, small beach access, easy to spot

Northshore Beaches

Tourist Lumahai

  • Activities to do:Swim/hangout/ small hike
  • Location:Northshore
  • Travel time from Lihue:30-45min
  • Overall rating: 8/10- fun beach to walk down to, easy beach access, limited activities, away from food places

Pinetrees

  • Activities to do: Bodyboarding/surfing
  • Location: Waioli County Beach Park
  • Travel time from Lihue: 30-45min
  • Overall rating: 8/10-great surfing sesh, sunsets are nice, open beach, easy access

Queens Bath

  • Activities to do:Cliff jumping/ small hike
  • Location:Kapiolani Loop, Princeville
  • Travel time from Lihue: 30-45min
  • Overall rating:6/10- fun to cliff jump when low tide, nice little pond area to swim, medium for beach access

Lihue beaches

Running Waters

  • Activities to do: Bodyboarding
  • Location: Lihue
  • Travel time from Lihue: 10-15min
  • Overall rating: 6/10-sometimes sticks in the water, nice waves, secluded, easy to hike to, showers are near the beach
